Program

Toddler's Program

Our toddler program focuses on self smoothing and self-regulation techniques, promoting independence, motor skills development, and coordination. Safari Kids Care, teachers track your toddlers progress through our daily communication sheets which provide individualized about what your child learned each day at school.

Educators

We believe that the success of our schools depend largely on an early childhood teachers. Our first requirement for our staff members is that they love what they do. We seek indiviudals who are team players and are dedicated to creating and maintaing a positive learning environment coupled with laugher and smiling faces.

Quality Rating and Accreditation

At Safari Kids Care, you can be confident that your child is receiving the best care and education possible. Our passionate teachers and research based curriculum meet early childhood content standards and best practices. Safari Kids Care demonstrates a commitment to quality care and education through accreditation and quality rating systems require us to meet rigorous early childhood education standards and guidelines for quality care. Pursuing accreditation and quality ratings is voluntary and demonstrates our ongoing commitment to meeting quality standards through excellence and education.

Parental Involvement

Safari Kids Care parent's involvement committee (PIC) allows parents to participate in the center by planning events that will benefit the overall quality of the program. Some of the group's initiatives include networking with other families, learning opportunities such as workshops, trainings, special events, community service projects, center enhancements, classroom volunteering opportunities, and fundraising.

Healthy Beginnings

We support your toddlers physical development by helping them meet developmental milestones such as crawling, standing, and walking. Through healthy beginnings our teachers help set and maintain individualized schedules to promote healthy eating and sleeping routines. This program blends educational concepts to promote health, fitness, and education. THrough activities and games, we inspire healthy beginnings that are designed to support your toddler's muscle development and coordination. This way we help your toddler's to master basic skills such as walking, running, hopping, jumping, kicking, and balancing.
