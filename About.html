Jeniffer Perez, Director

Hello everyone! Welcome! I’m Jeniffer, Director of Safari Kids Care located in Warren, MI. I graduated from Michigan State University with a bachelor’s degree in Human Development and Family Studies (GO GREEN!). I have more than 6 years of experience in childcare. I am CPR/ First Aid certified and my goal is to provide quality home childcare in a safe and nurturing environment. I attend trainings for licensing and the Great Start Quality program.

I love to travel! One of my favorite trips was spending time in the Bahamas! Fun fact, sting rays can weigh up to 1300 pounds!
