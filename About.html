Jeniffer Perez, Director

Hello Everyone! Welcome! I’m Jeniffer, Director of Safari Kids Care located in Warren, MI. I graduated from Michigan State University with a bachelor’s degree in Human Development and Family Studies (GO GREEN!). I have more than 6 years of experience in childcare. I am CPR/ First Aid certified. Safari Kids Care is a family-owned business with a passion for high quality preschool education where we focus on nurturing the whole child with their social, cognitive, gross motor and creative expressions. Our program is responsive to the needs of our children. My goal is to provide quality home childcare in a safe and nurturing environment. Entrusting your family childcare to a home daycare gives children the added security of being cared for in a home environment while still giving parents the peace of mind that comes from knowing their children are under the supervision of licensed professionals.
